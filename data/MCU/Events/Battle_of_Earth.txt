The Battle of Earth was the final large-scale confrontation between the restored Avengers and Thanos's forces in Endgame. The battle took place at the Avengers Compound after the use of time travel brought the Infinity Stones back into play and restored the possibility of reversing Thanos's victory. The event brought together heroes from across the MCU in a single desperate attempt to save the universe.

The battle included Tony Stark, Steve Rogers, Thor, Bruce Banner, Natasha Romanoff, Clint Barton, Captain Marvel, the forces of Wakanda, the Asgardian survivors, and many others. The conflict was massive, chaotic, and emotionally loaded because the heroes were fighting not only for victory but also for the lives and histories that had been stolen by the Snap. The battle demonstrated the full scale of the MCU's cooperative hero network.

The decisive result of the battle was Tony Stark's use of the Nano Gauntlet and his sacrifice to erase Thanos and his army. The battle ended Thanos's campaign and restored the universe's future, making it one of the most important moments in MCU history.
