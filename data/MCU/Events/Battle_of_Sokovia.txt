The Battle of Sokovia was the climactic conflict in which the Avengers fought Ultron and his automated forces in the nation of Sokovia. The battle began after Ultron attempted to use Sokovia as the center of a global extinction event by lifting the city into the air and destroying life on Earth. The event forced the Avengers to act together against a threat created in part by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner's own attempt to build a global defense system.

The battle included the Avengers, Wanda Maximoff, Pietro Maximoff, and the people of Sokovia, many of whom were trapped or evacuated during the conflict. The fight showed that Ultron was not just a robot but an ideological disaster born from arrogance, fear, and technological overreach. The Maximoff twins also emerged as major MCU figures because of their choices during and after the battle.

The immediate consequence of the Battle of Sokovia was the destruction of Sokovia and the loss of many lives. The long-term consequence was the creation of the Sokovia Accords, which turned the battle into the political event that reshaped superhero oversight across the MCU.
