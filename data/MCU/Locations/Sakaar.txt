Sakaar is a chaotic, junk-filled planet governed by the Grandmaster and known for gladiatorial combat, scavenging, and strange political freedom. Sakaar becomes important when Thor and Hulk are both drawn there and forced to survive in the planet's violent arena culture. The world is visually distinct from the rest of the MCU and functions almost like a cosmic dumping ground.

Sakaar matters because it becomes a place where Thor is stripped of his status and must adapt without Mjolnir or royal authority. Hulk's long absence from Earth also ties into Sakaar's role as a hidden off-world exile. The location is important as a contrast between imperial wealth, survival, and accidental self-discovery.
