Titan is Thanos's ruined homeworld and one of the most important off-world locations in the MCU. The planet's collapse shapes Thanos's philosophy and gives him the belief that universal imbalance must be corrected through extreme force. Titan later becomes the site of a major confrontation between Thanos and the heroes who try to stop him.

Titan matters because it connects Thanos's personal history to his cosmic mission. The battle on Titan shows the limits of heroism when the enemy already controls too much power, and it deepens the tragedy of Thanos's worldview.
