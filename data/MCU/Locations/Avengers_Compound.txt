The Avengers Compound is the post-Stark Tower headquarters used by the Avengers for training, planning, and crisis response. It serves as a private operational base where the team can coordinate without relying on outside governments or institutions. The Compound becomes a key location during the later stages of the MCU's conflict with Thanos.

The Avengers Compound matters because it symbolizes the team's attempt to create a stable home after the destruction and political conflict that followed earlier battles. The Compound is also the site of the final battle in Endgame, which gives the location a direct connection to the end of the Infinity Saga.
