Clint Barton, known as Hawkeye, is a master archer and longtime agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. who becomes one of the original Avengers. Clint Barton is distinguished by precision, discipline, and loyalty rather than superhuman powers. He is one of the first heroes to show that ordinary human skill can still matter in a world of gods, aliens, and enhanced beings.

Clint Barton is closely tied to Natasha Romanoff, and their partnership is one of the most important relationships in the MCU. Clint Barton also has a family life with Laura Barton and their children, which gives him a grounding presence outside the superhero world. His work with the Avengers places him in the Battle of New York, the Age of Ultron conflict, and the war against Thanos.

Clint Barton suffers intense personal loss during the Blip and becomes Ronin, a brutal masked vigilante who targets criminal organizations after his family disappears. This period shows how grief can distort Clint Barton's usually steady character. He later returns to the Avengers' cause and helps with the Endgame mission.

Clint Barton remains one of the MCU's most human heroes. His strengths are accuracy, calm under pressure, and a willingness to do difficult work without recognition. His relationship with Kate Bishop later extends his legacy into a mentoring role.
