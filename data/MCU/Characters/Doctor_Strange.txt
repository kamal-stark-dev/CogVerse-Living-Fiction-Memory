Stephen Strange, known as Doctor Strange, is the Sorcerer Supreme and one of the MCU's central magical heroes. Stephen Strange begins as a brilliant but arrogant surgeon whose career ends after a devastating car accident. Unable to recover through conventional medicine, Stephen Strange travels to Kamar-Taj and learns sorcery under the Ancient One, eventually becoming a protector of Earth against mystical threats.

Stephen Strange is closely connected to Wong, the Ancient One, Christine Palmer, and the Avengers. His relationship to magic is defined by discipline, sacrifice, and the recognition that reality is far larger than the world he once understood as a surgeon. Stephen Strange uses the Eye of Agamotto, later revealed to contain the Time Stone, and becomes a crucial participant in battles against Dormammu and Thanos.

Stephen Strange is often intellectually arrogant but grows into a character who understands the burden of responsibility. He is willing to break rules, manipulate time, and sacrifice personal certainty when the survival of the universe depends on it. Stephen Strange's role in Endgame makes him one of the most strategically important heroes in the MCU.
