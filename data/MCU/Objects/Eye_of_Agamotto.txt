The Eye of Agamotto is a mystical amulet used by Doctor Strange and originally associated with the sorcerers of Kamar-Taj. In the MCU it becomes the container for the Time Stone, turning it into one of the most powerful magical objects in the franchise. The Eye is both a symbol of the Sorcerer Supreme's authority and a practical instrument for time manipulation.

The Eye of Agamotto matters because it allows Doctor Strange to study, restrict, and eventually use the Time Stone in critical battles. The object plays a central role in the struggle against Dormammu and later against Thanos. Its history shows how magical artifacts in the MCU often combine spiritual symbolism with direct cosmic power.
