The Tesseract is a powerful cube-like artifact that contains the Space Stone and serves as one of the MCU's most important objects. The Tesseract passes through the hands of multiple characters and organizations, including the Asgardians, S.H.I.E.L.D., Loki, and Thanos. Its power over space makes it central to portals, travel, and cosmic conflict.

The Tesseract matters because it connects many of the MCU's earliest and largest events. Loki uses it to open the portal that leads to the Battle of New York, and Thanos later seeks it as part of his quest for all the Infinity Stones. The Tesseract is one of the clearest examples of how a single object can shape the entire timeline.
