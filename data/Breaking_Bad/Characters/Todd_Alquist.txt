Todd Alquist was a former exterminator for Vamonos Pest who became Walter White's laboratory assistant, cook, and the most unsettling villain in the Breaking Bad universe. Todd was born circa 1987, was soft-spoken, eager to please, and genuinely pleasant — and was the show's most chilling demonstration that evil does not require sadism, only the absence of a moral core.

Todd joined Walt and Jesse's operation when Vamonos Pest was selected as the front for their mobile meth lab. He participated in the methylamine train heist and, immediately upon its conclusion, shot and killed Drew Sharp, an eleven-year-old boy who had witnessed the operation, without warning or hesitation. His explanation was brief and almost cheerful: "No witnesses." He kept Drew Sharp's tarantula as a trophy.

What made Todd genuinely terrifying was his motivation: approval. He did not kill because he enjoyed cruelty or because he was greedy. He killed to please. He was deferential to Walt, obsessively attentive to Lydia Rodarte-Quayle, and uniformly polite to Jesse even as he held him prisoner. He gave Jesse ice cream after a successful cook. He brought Jesse soup during captivity. He apologized before having Andrea Cantillo executed in front of Jesse — "Just so you know, this isn't personal" — and then watched her fall to the ground without apparent disturbance. He was a blank, a void where empathy should have been.

After Walt and Jesse left the operation and Gus's empire collapsed, Todd became the primary cook for Jack Welker's gang in the new meth operation. His product peaked at 76% purity without the blue color — a significant drop from Walt's 99.1% — but Lydia considered it acceptable for their Czech Republic distribution network. Todd worked hard to improve his technique and took meticulous notes, channeling the same thorough diligence into drug production that others channel into hobbies.

Todd enslaved Jesse Pinkman after the Tohajiilee shootout, keeping him in a concrete underground cage and coercing his cooperation through the threat of harm to Andrea and Brock. When Jesse attempted to escape the compound, Todd executed Andrea at her front door as a punishment and a warning, while Jesse watched screaming from inside a car.

Todd was killed by Jesse Pinkman during Walt's machine-gun assault on the compound. Jesse strangled Todd with the chain of his own handcuffs, using the same method Walt had used on Krazy-8 in Season 1. Jesse's killing of Todd was an act of pure vengeance for Andrea, for Drew Sharp, and for the months of captivity and degradation — one of the series' most cathartic moments.

Todd Alquist's hidden cash stash, discovered by Jesse in a hollowed-out encyclopedia in his apartment after the massacre, provided Jesse with enough money to pay Ed Galbraith to relocate him to Alaska. Even in death, Todd inadvertently gave Jesse his freedom.
