## Moon Breathing is an exceptionally powerful and complex breathing style that serves as a counterpart to Sun Breathing. It was developed by Kokushibo, formerly known as Michikatsu Tsugikuni, after he transformed into a demon and joined Muzan Kibutsuji. Moon Breathing is characterized by its chaotic, crescent-shaped blade slashes that are chaotic and unpredictable, creating a wide range of varying-length attacks that constantly evolve in speed and intensity. Because Kokushibo is a demon, he combines this breathing style with his Blood Demon Art to manifest tangible, razor-sharp crescent blades that extend from his sword swings, making his combat range nearly insurmountable for average Demon Slayers.
