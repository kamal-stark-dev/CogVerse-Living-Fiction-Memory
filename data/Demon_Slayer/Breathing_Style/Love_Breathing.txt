## Love Breathing is a derivative of Flame Breathing, uniquely adapted to the physiology of its creator, Mitsuri Kanroji. It emphasizes agility, fluid movement, and long-range versatility. Because Mitsuri Kanroji uses a specialized, whip-like Nichirin blade, Love Breathing forms are characterized by rapid-fire, multidirectional strikes that allow her to strike enemies from a distance while maintaining a constant, rhythmic pace. The style blends the power of its parent style, Flame Breathing, with a flexibility that makes Mitsuri Kanroji difficult to pin down in battle.
