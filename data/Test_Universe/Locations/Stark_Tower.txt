Stark Tower is Tony Stark's iconic high-rise headquarters in New York City and one of the most recognizable locations in the early MCU. The tower reflects Tony Stark's public identity as a billionaire inventor and later becomes associated with the Avengers when the team begins to gather there. Stark Tower sits at the intersection of corporate power, advanced technology, and heroism.

Stark Tower matters because it marks the transition from individual heroics to team-based defense. The tower later becomes part of the Avengers' larger infrastructure, showing how Tony Stark's personal empire helps build the early superhero network.
